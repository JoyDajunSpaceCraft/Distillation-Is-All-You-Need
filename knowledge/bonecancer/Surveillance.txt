Surveillance

Description: Routine follow-up for osteosarcoma post-treatment
Actions for Surveillance

Physical Exam and Imaging

Perform physical exam and imaging of the primary site and chest.
Follow-Up Schedule

Every 3 months for year 1 and 2
Every 4 months for year 3
Every 6 months for year 4 and 5
Yearly thereafter, as clinically indicated
Laboratory Studies

Conduct complete blood count (CBC) and other laboratory studies as clinically indicated.
Advanced Imaging

Consider FDG-PET/CT (head-to-toe) and/or bone scan (category 2B).
Function Assessment

Reassess function at every visit.
