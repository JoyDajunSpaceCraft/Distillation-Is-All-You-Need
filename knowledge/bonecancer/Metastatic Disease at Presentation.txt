Metastatic Disease at Presentation

Question: Is the metastatic disease resectable or unresectable?
Resectable Metastatic Disease

Action:
Refer to OSTEO-2 for management of the primary tumor.
Consider chemotherapy, metastasectomy, stereotactic radiation therapy (RT), or ablation for pulmonary, visceral, and skeletal metastases.
Surveillance:
Follow up with surveillance according to OSTEO-4.
Unresectable Metastatic Disease

Action:
Administer chemotherapy and radiation therapy.
Reassess the primary site as appropriate for local control.
