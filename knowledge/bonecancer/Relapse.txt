Relapse

Description: Treatment options upon osteosarcoma relapse
Actions for Relapse

Chemotherapy and Resection

Administer chemotherapy and/or resection if possible.
Imaging to Assess Response

Conduct imaging to assess response, including X-rays, contrast-enhanced CT & MRI of local sites, and Chest CT.
Options for Relapse Progression

If there is relapse or progression, consider the following:
Resection, if possible
Participation in a clinical trial
Palliative radiation therapy (RT)
Best supportive care
